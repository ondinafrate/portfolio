TITLE: Massage Chair

CATEGORY: Industrial Design

DATE: July 2008

TOOLS: 3DSMax, House of Quality

COLLABORATORS: None

DESCRIPTION:

The 'Massage Chair' is my thesis project for my undergraduate degree in Industrial Design Engineering.

I started my project by doing research to learn which are the most important massage points in a human's body. After that, I started thinking of how the design would look like and sketched some ideas in 3DSMax.
