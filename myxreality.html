TITLE: Myx Reality

CATEGORY: Software, Product Development, UX/UI

DATE: August 2017

TOOLS: babylon.js, three.js, Sketch, Photoshop, node.js, InVision

COLLABORATORS: Joakim Quach

DESCRIPTION:

'Myx Reality' is a storyboarding tool for Virtual Reality Creators that minimizes time and labor to prototype and visualize ideas for VR.

PERSONAS & USER INTERVIEWS:

For this project I interviewed 212 VR creators to see what are their current way of storyboarding for virtual reality. We found out that creators still use pen and paper and they wrap it around their heads to see how this would look like in VR.



USER FLOW:

We imagined how our persona Steve would use our storyboarding software.



PROBLEM - SOLUTION APPROACH:

The problem was that the current method of storyboarding in VR was tedious and time consuming if they used pen and paper. If they would choose to prototype in VR all day, they said they would get a headache. Our solution was to build a platform that works on the browser and creators can esily storyboard their ideas by simply drag and drop assets onto the platform and test it in VR. This way, it's easier and they won't get a headache.
